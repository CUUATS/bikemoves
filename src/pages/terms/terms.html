User Agreement Safety

It is your responsibility as a cyclist to be safe and to follow the rules of the road. Always wear a helmet, and follow all applicable traffic laws.

Do not attempt to control the BikeMoves Illinois app while you are riding. Instead, find a safe place to stop before interacting with the app.

Privacy Policy

Demographics from your profile and any trips you submit will be shared with the Champaign County Regional Planning Commission and the Illinois Department of Transportation. We use your data for transportation planning purposes to improve bicycle facilities in the community.

We will not make public individual trips or other identifiable information. For details about the information we track and how we use it, see our Privacy Policy.

Terms and Conditions

You must be at least 13 years old to use BikeMoves.

BikeMoves allows you to record and submit bicycle trips and rider demographics. Once submitted, these data become the property of the Champaign County Regional Planning Commission (CCRPC) and the Illinois Department of Transportation (IDOT).

CCRPC and IDOT use data submitted via BikeMoves for planning purposes and may share data with partner agencies and, in aggregate form, with the public.
