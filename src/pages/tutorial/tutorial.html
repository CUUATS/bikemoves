Welcome to BikeMoves!

BikeMoves allows you to record your bicycle trips and share them with local planners. It also provides you with helpful information about the bicycle network.

Explore the Map

The map shows the locations of bicycle facilities like bike lanes and bike parking. Tap features on the map to learn more about them.

See a problem? Tap the + button to report issues like potholes or tree limbs blocking a bike lane to local officials.

Record a Trip

With automatic recording, BikeMoves will automatically start and stop trips based on the movement of your phone. Just open the app, jump on your bike, and go!

Automatic recording waits three minutes after you stop biking to make sure the trip is over. Tap the Stop button to skip the three minute delay and save the trip.

Upload Trips

Visit the Trips tab to review your saved trips. Tap Upload on a trip to share it with local planners.

Trips you upload help to improve bicycle planning in the community! To protect your privacy, the details of individual trips are never made public.

Track Your Riding

The Stats tab shows statistics about your trips, like the number of miles, average speed, and greenhouse gas reduced. You can view your riding statistics for the current week, month, or year.

Change Settings

The Settings tab allows you to enable or disable automatic recording and update your user profile. Demographics from your profile are shared anonymously with local planners to improve bicycle planning.

Need a refresher? You can always review this slideshow by tapping Help on the Settings tab.

Happy Riding!
